Structural Developments & Infrastructure

Our campus infrastructure supports learning, boarding and community life. Below are the main types of structural developments undertaken at Mauya Adventist High School.

Classrooms & Lecture Halls

Modern, ventilated classrooms with natural light and smart seating layouts to facilitate collaborative learning.

Dormitories & Boarding Houses

Safe, secure dormitories separated by gender with study areas, sanitation facilities and comfortable sleeping arrangements.

Science & Computer Laboratories

Equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and ICT to support hands-on practical learning and technology skills.

Dining Hall

A central dining hall with safe food preparation facilities that serve both students and staff.

Water & Sanitation

Reliable water supply, toilets and wash stations across campus to safeguard health and hygiene.

Energy & Solar Installations

Solar photovoltaic systems and backup power options to provide electricity for lights, ICT and essential services.

Access Roads & Drainage

Access roads, pathways and drainage works to ensure year-round access and prevent flooding on campus.

Sports Facilities & Assembly Areas

Play fields, courts and assembly spaces for physical education and community events.
